TEAMS Year 1 Collaboration Meeting

The first TEAMS collaboration meeting was held August 22-23 2018 in the J.R. Oppenheimer Study Center at Los Alamos National Laboratory as a satellite to the Radionuclide Astronomy in the 2020's and Beyond (RA2020) workshop.

Local organizers were Chris Fryer and Aimee Hungerford. For registration for RA2020 and/or our collaboration meeting, see the workshop website.
