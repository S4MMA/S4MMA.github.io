TEAMS 2020 Collaboration Meeting

Monday June 15, 2020 – Tuesday June 16, 2020

The SciDAC TEAMS 2020 Collaboration meeting will take place at Stony Brook University, hosted by the Institute for Advanced Computational Science.

You may register at the following link:
TEAMS Meeting Registration

  • There is no registration fee.
  • There is a limit of 40 participants—we may not be able to accommodate those registering late.
  • We will distribute talks among the different institutions, picking from those who indicated that they would like to give a talk.
