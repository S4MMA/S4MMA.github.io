S4MMA Workshop #1

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we have split our Project Scoping Workshop in to two parts. The first part is a virtual workshop to be held on December 9 and 11, 2020.

The program is divided into an opening discussion on s4MMA and related community activities, 8 topical working groups, and a concluding session to plan for next steps.
