Advances in Computational Science

Achieving TEAMS mission is impossible without the development of improved tools to model the astrophysical sites involved. To enable greater physical fidelity and accuracy, these codes must make efficient use of the coming generation of near-exascale and exascale platforms. This in and of itself is a challenging undertaking that requires considerable developments in computational science, both in new algorithms and in refactoring existing algorithms to take advantage of new hardware designs.
